• Indicates how good is the match to the best-matching locus.
  • Perfect: The K-locus region in your assembly is in one piece and has 100% coverage plus 100% nucleotide identity to the reference locus.
  • Very high: The K-locus region in your assembly is in one piece and has ≥99% coverage to the reference locus, with no expected genes missing and no unexpected K-locus genes.
  • High: The K-locus region in your assembly is in one piece and has ≥95% coverage to the reference locus, with ≤3 expected genes missing and no unexpected K-locus genes.
  • Good: The K-locus region in your assembly is in one piece OR has ≥95% coverage to the reference locus, with ≤3 expected genes missing and ≤1 unexpected K-locus genes.
  • Low: The K-locus region in your assembly is in one piece OR has ≥90% coverage to the reference locus, with ≤3 expected genes missing and ≤2 unexpected K-locus genes.
  • None: The K-locus region of your assembly does not meet the above criteria.
  • Expected gene = a gene in the best-match locus
  • Unexpected gene = a gene from another K-locus
