tBLASTn matches to genes from other K-locus references that were found outside the K-locus region of your assembly.
Several of the K-locus genes are homologous to genes found in other locations in the genome e.g. the rml or man genes. It is very common for Kaptive to find a small number of ‘other’ K-locus genes outside the K-locus region of the assembly. These are not a cause for concern and do not negatively impact the confidence of a K-locus match. However, if Kaptive finds a large number of genes (more than 5 or 6) the results should be treated cautiously.
